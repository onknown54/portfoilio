Hidden benefit of travelling experience

  • Olajide Shalom
  • Web Design
  • 14

When i was younger i loved travelling.In some ways it affected my life positively by giving me the opportunity to explore and have beautiful views. The little travelling experience i had helped me appreciate nature and beauty. In our system most parents use holiday seasons for children to attend summer schools and other less fun-filled activities which is not supposed to b that way. Travelling and going to new places to learn vocational studies would help kids more and help them have ideas on what to write in their "How i spent my long vacation essays". Here are following tips on how to have them a well spent holidays.

Listen to them: When i was a little kid i always wanted to go out, play with friends and try new things.But because of the job type and little amount of time they had, they couldn't help but to get us in summer school.This made us unhappy during the holiday periods and started driving out my adventurous spirit, by the time they realized i was used to being inside till now they can't even help it anymore.Ask where they would love to be, learn what their interest is.

Travel: This doesn't necessarily mean moving out of your state or out of your country.Take them to a place out of town talk to them,take many pictures ,let them see different buildings and structures, you would be surprised how it helps their IQ. Change their environment once in a while so when they will growthey wouldn't feel to strange to new places and new people Till date i still have a lot of travel sickness on the road just beacause i am so used to being home 24/7.

Learning new culture, learn how to eat new food, I remembered travelling to a place and i was welcomed by a lovely family ,they made their local dishes and i couldn't eat it ,it looked rude but i could not just bring my self to trying it ,it took several weeks for me to learn to appreciate their lovely meal. I for a person think parent should start considering this because they will not be with you forever, schol, work or marriage may let this child learn a new culture which might not be easy to blend into.

"Adventures are the best way to learn".

Let them learn how to bake, let them learn how to code, let them learn how to repair things, let them learn how to familiarize with people, and Channel their energy into advantageous things
